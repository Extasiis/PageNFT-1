Reasons why I am bullish

Professional Team

The Blade of God team is one of the most talented that exists from the CEO, founder, artist, moderators and game developers. Their great commitment has allowed us to have a maximum experience within the game as well as on the Discord server and its Twitter.

Art

The art design is amazing. As a designer I have evaluated every detail from the sketches and how they were built each of the characters to bring them to the game in 3D certainly have the highest score from my professional criteria.

Game

I think it is one of the strongest points, these last weeks I have spent the maximum from 4 to 6 hours playing and getting the maximum possible power to reach 12.7k. I clarify that it is not normal for a project to have such an advanced game pre mint to the point that it has generated a bullish feeling in me. The diverse game modes and the story inside are a gem. I firmly believe that the potential of Blade of God is gigantic.

United community

There is a wide variety of talented members with a lot of potential but mainly professional Gamers and Artists who improve the experience within the server. Teamwork is relevant especially when you need to create a caravan and play different campaigns with your friends and colleagues. I have certainly taken advantage of the opportunity to connect with several of them and create a strong friendship by supporting each other in and out of the game
